About

Hello and welcome to my website!

My name is Eric Gonnella, and I'm a content manager with a passion for website development, SEO, content creation, and marketing.

I'm 29 years old and have been working in this field for several years now. I started out by learning HTML and CSS, and I'm currently in the process of expanding my skillset by learning Javascript. I'm also experienced with WordPress, having built and managed multiple WordPress websites for various clients.

My expertise lies in creating websites that are both functional and visually appealing, with a focus on user experience and accessibility. Whether it's building a website from scratch or optimizing an existing site, I always strive to create a website that not only meets my clients' needs but also exceeds their expectations.

Aside from website development, I'm also experienced in SEO and content management. I know how important it is to create content that's both engaging and optimized for search engines, and I strive to create content that accomplishes both goals.

Thank you for visiting my website, and feel free to reach out if you have any questions or would like to work together on a project.
